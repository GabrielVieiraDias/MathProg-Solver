Linear Program in Two Variables

Write a MathProg model to find the maximum value of \(z=3x + 4y\ \ \) subject to the following set of constraints \[\begin{align} x + 2y & \leq 14 \\ 3x - y & \geq 0 \\ x - y & \leq 2 \end{align}\] The maximum value of \(z\) is 34. What are the corresponding values of \(x\) and \(y\)? What is the minimum value of \(z\)?
